Welcome!

My name is Abigail Marie Scarborough. I am a recent graduate from Eastern Washington University earning my BA degree in Technical Communication and a minor in Visual Communication Design.

Over my schooling, I have gained a strong passion for both writing and design. I hope to continue to excel in both these areas with the completion of my degree and professional experience as I move into the workforce post-graduation.

I am very independently motivated and always meet deadlines. On the same note, I am also very capable of working in a team setting. I have writing expertise in areas of instructional, proposal, usability, and technical editing. Design wise, I am experience in user-based document design, publication design, and designing for communication. I am familiar with numerous technical writing and design programs; such as Adobe CC applications and MadCap Flare to name a few.

I am 22 years old, and have lived in the small town of Nine Mile Falls, Washington all my life. I enjoy art, literature, baking, animal photography, and pet blogging.
